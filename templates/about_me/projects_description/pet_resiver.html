Description:

Pandamat is an automated device designed for receiving and preprocessing plastic bottles with a capacity of up to 2 liters. The primary process involves compressing the bottles using an integrated press, which reduces their volume for subsequent recycling. Upon completing the procedure, the device issues a receipt with a QR code containing information about the number of bottles received.
